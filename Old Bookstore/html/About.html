Introduction

In the past, people used to visit bookstores to buy a favorite book, but now with the development of the Internet, bookstores are gradually being forgotten and replaced by an online bookstore. However, the fact that most of these sites usually update new books and often ignore old books. Therefore, Reminiscence is an online bookstore selling old books so that people can come and enjoy the memory of a book of each person.

Mission

Reminiscence hopes to bring back the remembrance of the old but familiar books to customers so that they could immerse themselves into those ancient memories.

Commitment

Reminiscence commits that customers will receive books that have been checked, repaired, and packaged like the original so that you can fully feel the old memories perfectly.
